As of December 13, 2019, I’m proud to say I’m a Boston University alum!

These years have been filled with incredible memories—from living in [StuVi2](https://www.bu.edu/housing/residences/stuvi2/) to late-night study sessions and exploring Boston with some truly inspiring friends. It wasn’t always easy—balancing challenging courses, adapting during changing times, and navigating the infamous Boston winters—but it was all worth it, and I couldn’t have asked for a better college experience.

I’m grateful for the opportunities I had to learn, build, and grow, both in and outside the classroom. As I transitioned to full-time work, I was excited to apply everything I learned at BU to new challenges and opportunities.

If you’re a prospective student thinking about Boston University or a current student looking for advice, feel free to reach out to me via email or LinkedIn. I’m always happy to share what I’ve learned and help others on their journey!

Go Terriers! 🐾
